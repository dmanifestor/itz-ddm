Helpful Tips for Video SEO

Here are a few tips to get you started.

Content

Is your video content something people will find helpful, like how-to videos? Make your video content useful for consumers and the SEO will follow.

YouTube

Do you have a YouTube channel? Every created video should be uploaded to your channel. Then simply embed each video on your website.

Keywords

Discover important long-tail keywords that help you rank in the search results. Long-tail example: "How to grill a medium temperature ribeye steak."

Competitors

Are your competitors creating videos? How do they rank? If so, find out what keywords they use and make your video content better than theirs.

There are many other ranking factors regarding YouTube searches and Google Video searches. Although Google owns YouTube, the two algorithms are quite different. Here are some examples of how YouTube spends time learning about your audience.

  • What they watch
  • How many views your video gets
  • Average view duration
  • Impressions click-through rate
  • Ranking by views
