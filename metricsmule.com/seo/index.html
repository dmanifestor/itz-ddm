What is keyword research, and how do you do it?

Keyword research is the starting block for SEO. This is where you perform comprehensive research on what specific keywords are essential to you and your business and implement a strategy to get found in the search results when people search. For example: Let's say someone is searching for an automotive body shop. They might type something like "automotive body shop," automotive body shop near me," or "body shop with your city or zip code." They might even search for something like this: "how to choose the right body shop for my vehicle." Regardless of the searched keywords, we want to ensure you are found on the first page!
